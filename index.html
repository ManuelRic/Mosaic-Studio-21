02 / From photo to plan

How it works

Seven values. Two faces. One image.

01

Choose your source

Upload a clear photo or type a short phrase. Everything is processed locally in your browser.

02

Map light to pips

Each half is sampled and translated into one of seven tonal values, from blank to six pips.

03

Export your blueprint

Download a crisp PNG, note the exact grid, and use the inventory to prepare a physical build.

Studio tip

High-contrast portraits, simple silhouettes, and short bold phrases make the clearest mosaics.
